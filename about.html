About Us

Chilliwack Children's Dentistry is a pediatric dental practice serving Chilliwack, Sardis and the surrounding Fraser Valley, brought to the community by the team behind Ridge Meadows Children's Dentistry.

We see the full range of young patients, from infants and toddlers through children, teens and kids with additional needs, with a focus on preventive care, interceptive orthodontics and gentle corrective treatment when it is needed.

How we work

Dr. Anu and the team aim for a family-friendly atmosphere that shapes every part of your child's dental care. Our goal is a positive, compassionate and inclusive experience, the kind that builds a lasting relationship rather than a one-off appointment. We treat a child's dental health as part of their overall health and wellbeing, not something separate from it.

Day to day, that covers examinations and cleanings, fillings, sealants, sedation care, emergency trauma management and interceptive orthodontics. For anxious or hesitant kids, we start with simple behaviour guidance techniques and only move toward mild or moderate sedation, or a general anesthesia consultation, when it is genuinely needed. The aim throughout is a relaxed, unhurried visit for every child who walks through our door.

Meet your dentist

Dr. Anu Korada

Dr. Anu trained in the UK and moved to Canada from London with her family. A mother of two herself, she brings more than ten years of experience in pediatric and special care dentistry across Canada and the UK, and she teaches pediatric dentistry at UBC and BC Children's Hospital.

She believes good oral hygiene is the gateway to good overall general health, and she is focused on giving children positive experiences and habits they carry with them into adulthood.
